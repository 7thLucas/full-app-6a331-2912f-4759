# Product Overview

## Product Name
**Clinic Coordinator**

## What It Is
A unified clinic management application built for small clinic administrators. It centralises patient records, appointment scheduling, and prescription management into a single streamlined workflow, reducing administrative friction and enabling staff to deliver faster, higher-quality care.

## Primary Users
- **Clinic Administrators** — the primary operators; manage scheduling, patient flow, record-keeping, and daily clinic coordination
- **Doctors / Practitioners** — access patient records, review visit history, and issue prescriptions during consultations
- **Support Staff** — check patients in, update statuses, and assist with record maintenance

## Core Problem
Small clinics typically juggle patient records, appointments, and prescriptions across disconnected systems — paper files, basic spreadsheets, or entirely separate tools — creating:
- Long patient wait times from slow check-in and manual record lookups
- Prescription errors due to incomplete or siloed patient history
- Scheduling conflicts and missed or double-booked appointments
- No real-time visibility into daily patient flow and clinic capacity

## Solution & Positioning
A single admin hub that connects every step of the patient journey — from booking through check-in, record access, consultation, and prescription — so clinic staff spend their time on patients, not paperwork.

## Core Features
1. **Patient Records Management** — create, search, and update patient profiles including medical history, allergies, and contact information
2. **Appointment Scheduling** — book, reschedule, and cancel appointments with real-time slot availability and automatic conflict detection
3. **Prescription Management** — issue, track, and review prescriptions tied directly to patient records and consultation history
4. **Patient Flow Dashboard** — a live view of today's appointments and patient status (waiting / in-consultation / done) with urgent alerts

## Strategic Goals
- Reduce patient wait times across all clinic touchpoints
- Improve care quality through complete, instantly accessible patient information
- Give small clinics a professional-grade system without enterprise complexity or cost

## Brand & Tone
- **Feel**: Calm, clean, trustworthy, professional
- **Audience**: Non-technical clinic administrators and practitioners in small, independent clinics
- **Aesthetic**: Healthcare-appropriate — clear typography, restrained colour, efficient layouts

## Scope (MVP) — Shipped
- Patient records (create, view, edit, search; medical history, allergies, emergency contacts)
- Appointment scheduling (day-navigator view, booking, conflict detection, one-click status transitions)
- Prescription issuance and history (multi-medication, lifecycle tracking, linked to patient records and appointments)
- Daily patient flow dashboard (live status tracking, urgent-patient alerts, quick-action links)
- Authentication (JWT-based login/logout, role-based access, auto-seeded admin account)
- Clinic configurables (clinic name, tagline, address, working hours, slot duration, prescription feature flag)
