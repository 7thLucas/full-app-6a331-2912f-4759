# Clinic Coordinator

## What It Is
A unified clinic management application built for small clinic administrators. It centralises patient records, appointment scheduling, and prescription management into a single streamlined workflow, reducing administrative friction and enabling staff to deliver faster, higher-quality care.

## Primary Users
- Clinic Administrators — manage scheduling, patient flow, record-keeping, and daily clinic coordination
- Doctors / Practitioners — access patient records, review visit history, and issue prescriptions during consultations
- Support Staff — check patients in, update statuses, and assist with record maintenance

## Core Problem
Small clinics juggle patient records, appointments, and prescriptions across disconnected systems — paper files, spreadsheets, or separate tools — creating long wait times, prescription errors, scheduling conflicts, and no real-time visibility into patient flow.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. Patient Records Management — create, search, and update patient profiles including medical history, allergies, and contact information
2. Appointment Scheduling — book, reschedule, and cancel appointments with real-time slot availability and automatic conflict detection
3. Prescription Management — issue, track, and review prescriptions tied directly to patient records and consultation history
4. Patient Flow Dashboard — live view of today's appointments and patient status (waiting / in-consultation / done) with urgent alerts

## Strategic Goals
- Reduce patient wait times across all clinic touchpoints
- Improve care quality through complete, instantly accessible patient information
- Give small clinics a professional-grade system without enterprise complexity or cost

## Brand & Tone
- Feel: Calm, clean, trustworthy, professional
- Audience: Non-technical clinic administrators and practitioners in small, independent clinics
